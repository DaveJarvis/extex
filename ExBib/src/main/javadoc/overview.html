εχTEX is a bibliography processor developed in the context of εχTEX.

εχBib

εχTEX aims at providing a high-quality typesetting system. The development of εχTEX has been inspired by the experiences with TEX. The focus lies on an open design and a high degree of configurability. A tight integration of several components is one of the possibilities opened by εχTEX. To work into this direction εχBib has been implemented. It is a bibliography processor and can also be used as plug-in replacement for BIBTEX 0.99c or BIBTEX 8.
