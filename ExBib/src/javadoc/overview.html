εχBib is a bibliography processor developed in the context of εχTeX.

εχTeX aims at providing a high-quality typesetting system. The development of εχTeX has been inspired by the experiences with εχTeX. The focus lies on an open design and a high degree of configurability. A tight integration of several components is one of the possibilities opened by εχTeX. To work into this direction εχBib has been implemented. It is a bibliography processor and can also be used as plug-in replacement for BIBTeX 0.99c or BIBTeX 8.
