#ExTeX() Sub-Projects

The scope of #ExTeX() has been extended and the different parts have been restructured into sub-projects. The sub-projects provide useful functionality of their own. In addition they are meant as modules for an advanced typesetting environment. The following sub-projects exist:

  • #ExTeX() – the Typesetter
  • #ExBib() – the Bibliography Processor
  • #ExIndex() – the Index Processor
